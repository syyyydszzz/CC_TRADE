# Project Background

In this project, you will develop an algorithmic trading strategy utilizing the ALGOGENE platform. The goal is to create, test, and optimize a trading algorithm that has a potential to execute in the real market.

Suppose you are now a quant researcher working in a hedge fund. You are asked to develop a new trading strategy for potential investors. Five years of 1-minute data from 2021 to 2025 are provided, including market data, news, economic statistics, etc. You can freely design your trading strategy, in terms of the mathematical models, input datasets, trading instruments, etc. Please design a trading algorithm and summarize your results and findings using the report template below.

If your strategy relies on other external data or custom models, you may refer to the following articles to import to the system for backtesting:

- Guideline to backtest with custom datasets
- Guideline to import custom package
- Guidelines to re-use model results for backtests
